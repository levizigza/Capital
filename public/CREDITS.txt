Figure & industrial reference (non-asset)
-----------------------------------------
Humanoid proportions for Capital Voyager / Harbor locals were refined using
figure-drawing practice conventions (e.g. tools like Figurosity.com for pose and
head-count ratios). Dock hardware language (cleats, bolt collars, metal rails)
is original geometry inspired by industrial fastener catalogs such as McMaster-Carr
— no third-party CAD files were imported.
Free 3D marketplaces (e.g. free3d.io / Poly Pizza CC0 packs) informed the
stylized low-poly direction; Capital ships original procedural meshes plus the
Kenney Nature Kit subset above.
